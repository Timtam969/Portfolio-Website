Welcome to my Portfolio Page!

I was born in South Africa, completing my schooling up to Matric level. We lived in various places during my 20 years there, including Rustenburg and Botswana. When I was 20 years old I moved to England where I lived for 6 years before moving to Australia. Having started my diving interest in South Africa, I decided to spend the next 3 years learning the trade in one of the busiest diving destinations in the World. The Great Barrier Reef. Once I have all my qualifications and quite a bit of experience behind me, it was time to move over to the Philippines, where I ran my own private resort for 4 years as well as taking on the task of being a partner in SSI PHilippines and overseeing the develoment of the diving Industry in the Area. Shortly after I met my wife and we started our amazing family. This brought on the next big change for us, moving to Germany.
